The ability of the hip implant to support and distribute weight.

Surgical technique involving smaller incisions and reduced recovery time.

Adjustable component connecting the femoral stem to the femoral head.

Hip implant with interchangeable components for customization.

Oscillating Saw

Surgical saw that moves back and forth to cut bone.

Osteointegration

The process of bone growing into and integrating with an implant or prosthesis.

Bone resorption or loss around an implant, often due to wear debris.

Surgical tool used to cut or shape bone.

Surgical procedure to cut and reshape bone to correct alignment.
