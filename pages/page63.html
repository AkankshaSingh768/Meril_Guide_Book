Lateral Approach

Surgical approach from the side of the body.

Lateral Lumbar Interbody Fusion (LLIF)

Fusion of lumbar vertebrae through a lateral approach with an interbody device.

Lordosis

Natural inward curve of the lumbar spine.

Lumbar Spine

The portion of the spine in the lower back.

Minimally Invasive Surgery (MIS)

Surgery performed with smaller incisions to reduce recovery time.

Monaxial Screw

Screw with a single axis of motion for fixed alignment.

Navigation System

Technology used to guide surgical instruments with high precision.

Nerve Hook

Tool used to gently manipulate nerves during surgery.

Nerve Retractor

Tool used to retract nerves to provide a clear view during surgery.

Nerve Root

The initial segment of a spinal nerve emerging from the spinal cord.

Nucleus Pulposus

Gel-like core of the intervertebral disc that provides cushioning.
